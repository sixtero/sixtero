Welcome to Sixtero 👋, a community for everyone who wants to live closer to nature.

📚 Our platform offers a wiki and a space for sharing ideas, experiences, and inspiration.

The word Sixtero was created by combining "six" and "tero" (a suffix from the Czech collective numeral "šestero"). Sixtero thus refers to six fundamental pillars that form our approach to living closer to nature:

- 🌱 Naturally - in harmony with nature
- 🔄 Regularly - with consistency and discipline
- 🧘 Consciously - with full awareness and presence
- 💛 Compassionately - with empathy and consideration
- 😊 Joyfully - with joy and a positive attitude
- 🦁 Courageously - with courage and determination

_Start naturally, do it regularly, be conscious about it, be compassionate to others, experience it joyfully, and don't be afraid to go against the current._

🤝 Join us and discover how you can live closer to nature every day! 🌿🌍
